Whatsapp chat uploader

Organising on WhatsApp? Sharing links to important information on a chat? Want a way to quickly download all the links that are useful to your community?

Our WhatsApp link extractor is a tool that makes it easy to export and download all the useful links from your WhatsApp chat history. Here's how it works in 3 easy steps!

  1. First, you need to export your WhatsApp chat history. On Android, you can do this by going to the chat settings, selecting the chat you want to export, and then selecting "Export Chat."
  2. On an iPhone, you can do this by going to the chat settings, selecting "Export Chat," and then selecting "Export Chat History." Note that in both cases, the exported chat will be saved in the form of a text file to your device. You can then share this file with others or save it for your own records.
  3. Next, upload your chat history to our tool online using the button below! Our link extractor tool will scan the chat for links.
  4. Finally, click the button to download the list of links to your computer!

You can then use the list of links to do whatever you need with them! It's that easy!
